What to select?

Select at least two base materials for creating blended materials. After selection configure mixture ratios in terms of minimal and maximal values of each base material and the increments which define the step size from minimal to maximal value. The last material's configuration will be determined automatically by the values used in the other base materials. Values above 100% in total as well as negative values are not allowed.
